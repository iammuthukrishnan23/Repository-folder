Welcome To AKZ Explore Global

AKZ Explore Global. Ltd is a subsidiary of AKZ Group of Hotels, one of the biggest hotel chains in south Tamil Nadu. The AKZ Group of Hotels founded in 1998 has for forty years provided impeccable service and outstanding hospitality. It currently has 19 properties in Tamil Nadu. AKZ Explore Global Pvt. Ltd built on this strong foundation aims to provide great customer satisfaction and an exemplary holiday experience. Planning a once in a lifetime holiday or a yearly corporate retreat? No problem! AKZ Explore Global can get you what you want and in the minimal time. One of the best destination management companies in South India, it has the experience and infrastructure to handle any customer demand. In addition to destination management, AKZ Explore Global also plans corporate tours, incentive trips, college/school excursions, business travel, arranges car/railway & hotel bookings and much, much more.
